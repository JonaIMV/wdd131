About me

I started playing rugby thanks to a bet with my brother-in-law after seeing it on TV.
The following week, in 1984, I attended my first training session at Fort Worth Rugby Club in Fort Worth, Texas.
I played there for 11 years. Later, we moved to San Antonio, Texas, where I played with the San Antonio Rugby Club from 1995 to 2000.
After moving to Austin, Texas, I played with the Austin Huns Rugby Club from 2000 to 2007 in Division 1.
I coached Division 2 and Division 3 teams from 2006 to 2009, when I moved to Puerto Morelos.
From 2009 to 2015, I coached the Cancún Hammerheads.
In 2015, I was the coach for the Quintana Roo women's U19 team at the National Olympics in Monterrey, where we won the bronze medal.
In 2009, I started the youth rugby program “Leopardos Rugby Puerto Morelos.”
Currently, I serve as the Technical Director for Leopardos Rugby Puerto Morelos and Toros Rugby Infantil (2024).
In 2025, I became the representative of the organization called “Dilo Asociación de Rugby Juvenil” AC.
I am a Level 1 Certified Coach and Level 1 Certified Referee with World Rugby Union.
